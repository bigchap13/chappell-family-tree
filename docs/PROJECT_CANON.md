## Product direction

Chappell Family Tree is not a lightweight family-tree diagram. It is a complete family-history and genealogy platform designed to preserve people, relationships, records, evidence, media, locations, memories, and research.

## Evidence rules

1. Never invent a person, date, location, relationship, or historical event.
2. Store uncertain information as unverified.
3. Preserve the original source URL, archive, collection, record identifier, access date, and transcription.
4. Separate facts from theories and research leads.
5. Record conflicting evidence rather than silently choosing one version.
6. Protect information about living people by default.
7. Do not scrape subscription services or bypass authentication.
8. Search and use freely accessible public records only where permitted.

## Flagship interface

The application must use:

- A centered premium top header
- Large phone-readable text
- Expandable maternal and paternal branches
- Smooth tree navigation
- Profile cards with clear relationships
- Search, timeline, map, records, media, and research sections
- Dark premium visual design
- Consistent ChapNetAI branding

## Future AI feature

The future AI component will be called the AI Genealogy Companion.

It may:

- Summarize sourced family biographies
- Identify missing information
- Detect possible duplicate people
- Explain relationships
- Suggest research paths
- Compare conflicting records
- Generate source-aware family reports

It must never present an unsupported guess as fact.
