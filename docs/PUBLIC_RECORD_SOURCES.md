# Public Record Source Registry

Initial research categories:

- Federal census records
- State census records
- Birth indexes
- Death indexes
- Marriage records
- Divorce records
- Cemetery records
- Obituaries
- Military service records
- Draft registrations
- Immigration records
- Passenger lists
- Naturalization records
- Probate records
- Wills
- Land and deed records
- Court records
- Church registers
- School records
- City directories
- Historical newspapers
- Historical maps
- Government archives
- County genealogy projects
- User-owned family documents
- GEDCOM imports

Every future source adapter must document:

- Provider
- Access method
- Cost
- Authentication requirements
- Search limitations
- Reuse permissions
- Citation format
- Privacy considerations
